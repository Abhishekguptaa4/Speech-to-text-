About Us

Sound to text Are you looking for a way to generate transcripts of your voice overs, podcasts or meetings quickly and easily? Look no further! The free audio to text converter helps you generate transcripts of your audio recordings and conversations quickly and easily in minutes. And the best part is that it all runs in your web browser so you don’t have to worry about downloading or installing anything to your computer. Just log in, upload your audio, click the Transcribe button and sit back while our software gives you a perfect transcript of the audio that you can then edit and save to your device!.

How to convert audio to text:-

Step 1: Upload To start converting your audio to text with this website, just click the Transcribe or Get Started buttons . Then, drag your audio files over to the browser window or press the “click to upload” b

Step 2: Transcribe After the file has uploaded it will be processed and the transcription will show up on the right side of the screen. If needed you can also make changes.

Step 3: Download your script by click on the download button.
